PROJECT BUSINESS SERVICE

BONZAI™ is a fictional business service created for the purpose of a master's project for course ISM 6255 (Distributed Information Systems). BONZAI™ was inspired by the Japanese meaning of Bonsai. Bonsai is an asian art form using cultivation techniques to produce small trees. Bonsai trees are known for its resilience and its ability grow in obscure places given the right conditions. Likewise, this business service BONZAI™, could provide guidance to its customers by providing insights that could generate income even in a high volatility market. In a real-world application, we would apply machine learning and AI to discover dividend stocks suited to the customer's needs. The deployed application utilizes IEX Trading APIs. The web app generates list of long term best dividend stocks, list of best dividend stocks by sector, list of company's top picks and allows the customer to search stocks.
